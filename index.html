Report Scam Crypto Wallets.

Help Stop Crypto Crime. Protect Others.

Wallet Reporters provides a secure way to report cryptocurrency wallets used in scams and route that information to law enforcement, regulators, and exchanges.

  • We do not recover funds.
  • We do not contact victims.
  • We do not ask for private keys.
Search the Registry

A reported wallet does not imply wrongdoing. It indicates a wallet has been submitted for review based on a user report and blockchain activity.

How it works
  1. Submit the wallet(s) you sent funds to
  2. We generate an automated tracing report
  3. Reports are routed automatically (no direct police contact)
Disclaimer: We do not contact police departments or investigating officers directly. Reports are generated and delivered automatically by our system.
